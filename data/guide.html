SUPERVISOR RESPONSIBILITIES
  1. To ASSIST users in using PRINTING services provided by Campus Supplies
  2. To ASSIST users with the use of IT facilities provided by NUSSU CommIT
  3. To ensure that there must be at least one supervisor ready at the supervisor's desk during operating hours at all time
  4. To look after NUSSU CommIT properties
  5. To enforce and adhere strictly to the rules and the regulation of the centers
  6. To maintain an acceptable level of cleanliness and order in the centers
  7. To INFORM center manager/technical manager of any problems in the centers and report any equipment faults in the shortest possible time
  8. To uphold a professional image of NUSSU CommIT and maintain a high spirit of service at all times

DOs AND DON'Ts FOR SUPERVISORS
  1. Be PUNCTUAL for duty!! Arrive at centers 5 minutes before duty.
  2. Supervisor must not be away from the place of duty for more than 15 minutes.
  3. No sleeping during duty.
  4. Only supervisors on duty or NUSSU CommIT Main Committee member are allowed to enter the counter. Sub-Comm members not on official business are NOT allowed behind the counter.
  5. Always wear the duty T-shirt while on duty.
  6. All supervisors on duty are to observe strict codes of conduct and behave appropriately. Inappropriate behavior will not be tolerated.
  7. Be vigilant and alert when on duty. Be pro-active and approach students who appear lost or in need of assistance.
  8. Should there be and disciplinary problems/complaints, the NUSSU CommIT Main Committee will carry out a through inquiry and reserve the right to mete out any punishments or penalties as it deems just and appropriate.

DUTY PROCEDURES
  1. Supervisor is to sign on both the CBLC Supervisor Sign In/Out Book and his own Claim Form to verify attendance.

OPENING AND CLOSING OF CENTER
Opening
  1. CL: Get the keys from the Librarian Reception desk's drawer.
  2. Switch on monitor for booking system & print job release.
  3. Switch on the lights, printers, release station monitors.
  4. Ensure that the center is clean.

REPLACEMENT OF TONERS
  1. When the printer indicates “low toner” on the LCD panel, DO NOT change the toner immediately. ONLY change toner when the printer is out of ink.
  2. Replace the toner only if the printouts become faded.
  3. Print the usage form and place in the folder.
    Usage form: OK -> Information -> Usage page (for A4 BW printers)

PC STATUS CHECKING GUIDE
Problem: The system could not log you on. The NUS*** domain is not available.
Proposed Solution:
  1. Ensure the LAN cable is fully both plugged into the LAN port in the PC and the wall socket.
  2. Restart the system/
  3. If the above solution does not work, contact operations manager.
Problem: The monitor fails to power on.
Proposed Solution:
  1. Check that the ON/OFF switch is ON
  2. Ensure the power cable is properly connected to the monitor.
  3. Try to power on again.
  4. If problem persists, contact operations manager.
Problem: The mouse/keyboard fail to work.
Proposed Solution:
  1. Check that the mouse/keyboard is fully plugged into the PC's respective port.
  2. Restart the system.
  3. If problem persist, contact operations manager.
For problems listed below, please contact the CBLC Duty and Technical & Operations Manager.
  • User profile cannot be loaded. Please contact your administrator.
  • The PC fails to boot up and indicates that a critical file is missing/corrupted.
  • The PC fails to boot up by displaying the booting process screen and any other screen constantly.
  • The BLUE SCREEN OF DEATH (BSOD) appears constantly.
  • The monitor fails to display anything.
  • Any other problems.

RELEASE STATION TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE
Problem: User has sent his/her print jobs, but not shown on the release station.
Proposed Solution:
  1. Ensure that the user key in the correct NUSNET USER ID.
    • Same USER ID with his/her PC terminal
    • Matric No. without the last alphabet.
  2. At the user's PC, ensure that the print job has been sent to the printer.
  3. Try refreshing the Release Station.
Problem: The spooling in the process screen appears showing “Please wait while the Printing manager is releasing your print job”.
Proposed Solution:
  1. Refresh the system. Press Alt+U to check whether the print job is being sent over.
  2. If the job is not being sent, and spooling is displayed for more than 5 minutes, move the cursor to the extreme left and press Alt+5.
Problem: The cash card cannot be read.
Proposed Solution:
  1. Clean the chip on the cash card either by rubbing it with finger or rubber.
  2. Use sand-papered cash card and insert it into the reader several times to scrub the sensor surface in the machine.
Any other problem please call Daren (9428 7324)

How to read the Data Log
Example:
08:48:37, U0605213, OB$3.78,          NB$3.28,     Cost$0.50,     B-W-A4,              languages (1).doc, pages 10      ($0.50)
Time,     User ID,  original balance, new balance, deducted cost, printer released to, filename,          no. of pages, cost of document.
Note: User might print more than 1 file in one go. Thus, there will be multiple entries in the data log. You would have to total up the cost for all the user's print jobs.
